Who We Are

Fair Connects India

FAIR CONNECTS INDIA is a next-generation exhibition organizing company based in Chennai, India. We bring together industry knowledge, professional project management and innovative marketing to deliver exhibitions that matter.

Our leadership team and associates have extensive experience — more than 20 years — in managing large-scale trade fairs, conferences and B2B networking events across multiple industry verticals.

At FCI, we specialize in conceptualizing and executing premium exhibitions for high-growth sectors of the Indian economy. With a strategic, data-driven approach and world-class execution standards, we help industries showcase innovation, expand markets, and build long-term partnerships.

Our Vision

A Trusted Name in India's Exhibition Industry

To be a leading exhibition organizer in India, recognized for credibility, quality execution, and value-driven industry platforms. We envision FCI as the go-to partner for industries seeking to showcase their innovations and connect with their ideal markets.
